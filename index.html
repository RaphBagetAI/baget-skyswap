About SkySwap

SkySwap connects amateur astronomers with verified hosts who share their telescopes for local meetups. Whether you're a beginner, a family, or an experienced stargazer, find trusted nearby meetups to borrow telescope access, learn quickly, and enjoy the night sky together.
